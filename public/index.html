Frequently Asked Questions

What is ConvoNote?

ConvoNote is an AI-powered web app that turns your meeting audio into punctuated transcripts and clear summaries. It helps individuals and teams instantly extract insights from conversations and download them in a clean document.

How does ConvoNote work?

Upload any meeting audio file (MP3 or WAV). ConvoNote automatically transcribes it, fixes punctuation, formats it line-by-line, and generates a brief summary using GPT-like intelligence. Then, you can download everything as a professional document.

Is ConvoNote free?

Yes! ConvoNote is free to use with your own OpenAI or Azure API key. We use your credentials securely so you can control your usage and costs.

What formats are supported?

ConvoNote supports .wav and .mp3 files up to 25MB in size.

Does ConvoNote store my data?

No. Your audio is processed temporarily for transcription and summary, then deleted. We don‚Äôt permanently store any uploaded files or content.

Can I use it for interviews or lectures?

Absolutely. ConvoNote is ideal for meetings, interviews, lectures, webinars, and any scenario where you want clean transcripts and summaries without writing notes.

What makes ConvoNote different?

ConvoNote not only transcribes ‚Äî it intelligently adds punctuation, breaks lines for clarity, and summarizes your conversations with GPT-like language models. All this is packed into a fast, responsive, modern UI with instant PDF export.
